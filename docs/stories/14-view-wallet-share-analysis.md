# User Story: 14 - View Wallet Share Analysis

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to see wallet share analysis for high-value clients,
**so that** I can identify opportunities to deepen relationships and capture more of their investable assets.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Dashboard shows estimated wallet share for each high-value client
* Visual indicator of current share vs. potential opportunity (held-away assets)
* Calculated based on aggregated **Total Wealth** (AUM + external investments)
* Comparison across client segments (Family Offices, SWFs, CXOs)
* Highlights clients with low wallet share but high potential
* Wallet share trends over time are trackable in USD (Millions/Billions)
* Integrates with lead scoring to prioritize outreach

## Notes

* Page 1 highlights: "Wallet share analysis for high-value clients"
* Helps RMs understand percentage of customer's total investments captured
* Focus on UHNW individuals and Family Offices with $1B+ AUM potential
