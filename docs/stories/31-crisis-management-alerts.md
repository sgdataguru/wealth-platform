# User Story: 31 - Crisis Management Alert System

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to receive "Immediate Action" alerts triggered by geopolitical or market crises,
**so that** I can proactively communicate with clients and protect their wealth during volatile events.

## Acceptance Criteria

* **Crisis Management Alerts** appear in the "Immediate Action" section of the dashboard.
* Alerts are triggered by specific events: Geopolitical instability (GCC focus), market crashes, or major regulatory shifts.
* Each alert includes a summary of affected clients and a "one-tap" communication template.
