# User Story: 29 - GCC Regulatory Compliance (SAMA/DIFC/ADGM)

**As a** Compliance Officer,
**I want** the platform to generate reports and alerts aligned with GCC-specific regulations (SAMA, DIFC, ADGM, CMA),
**so that** the firm maintains full compliance with Middle Eastern authorities and avoids penalties.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Pre-built regulatory reporting templates for:
  * **SAMA** (Saudi Central Bank)
  * **DIFC** (Dubai International Financial Centre)
  * **ADGM** (Abu Dhabi Global Market)
  * **CMA** (Capital Market Authority - Saudi Arabia/Oman)
* Real-time monitoring of client transactions against regional AML and KYC thresholds.
* Automated flagging of "Cross-border" transactions requiring specific disclosures.

## Notes

* Terminology specific to transcript: ADGM, CMA, SAMA, DIFC.
* Supports the requirement for an on-premises solution for strict compliance.
